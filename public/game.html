How to Play

⚔️
Deploy Face-Up
Play a card to its matching region. Its ability activates immediately (Instant) or stays active (Ongoing).
🌑
Deploy Face-Down
Play any card face-down to any region. It counts as strength 2 with no ability. Right-click a region, or use the Hidden Deploy button.
🏳️
Retreat
Concede the round. Your opponent scores VP based on how many cards they have left in hand.
👑
Win Condition
First to 12 Victory Points wins. Each round, the player who controls the majority of regions (2 of 3) scores 6 VP.
📍
Regions
Noxus · Demacia · Ionia are arranged left to right. Adjacent regions matter for several card abilities.
🔄
Initiative
The loser of each round goes first next round. Ties are broken in favour of the initiative holder.
