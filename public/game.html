How to Play

⚔️
Deploy Face-Up
Play a card to its matching region. Ability activates immediately (Instant) or stays active (Ongoing).
🌑
Deploy Face-Down
Play any card face-down to any region. Counts as strength 2, no ability. Right-click a region or use the Hidden Deploy button.
🏳️
Retreat
Concede the round. Opponent scores VP based on their remaining hand size.
👑
Win Condition
First to 12 Victory Points wins. Controlling the majority of regions (2 of 3) scores 6 VP each round.
📍
Regions & Adjacency
Noxus · Demacia · Ionia — left to right. Adjacent regions matter for many abilities.
🔄
Initiative
Round loser goes first next round. Ties broken by initiative holder.
